Introduction

The E-Summit will be the annual flagship event of the Entrepreneurship Development Cell, Birla Institute of Technology, Mesra in association with other clubs/societies/cells of the college in order to foster the entrepreneurial / intrapreneurial activities in the campus. The E-Summit aims at bringing investors, venture capitalists, industrial experts and the academic community and all those entrepreneurial enthusiasts into one common ground.

Panel Discussions

Platform engaging speakers from across the Industry sharing their thoughts and ideas on latest developments in Science, Technology and Innovation.

Industry Collaborations

To create a holistic environment to enrich and help existing and budding entreprenuers connect with the Industrial scenario and its latest requirements.

Academic Partnership

To bring onboard a variety of thoughts and ideas from various academic institutions and to create sustainable and innovative business model.

Speaker Sessions

Fast paced sessions to provide students and entreprenuers with techniques and strategies to enrich their entreprenuerial journey.

Industrial academia meet

E-Summit aims at developing more crafted leaders for next generation through various guest lectures, workshops, and competitions for all the participants, audience and organizers.
