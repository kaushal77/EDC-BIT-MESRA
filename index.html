ENTERPRENEURSHIP DEVELOPMENT CELL BIT MESRA



Entrepreneurship Development Cell was established in 2007 under the aegis of STEP (Science and Technology Entrepreneurs’ Park). The Entrepreneurship Development Cell is a non-profit organization run by the students supported by administration of BIT Mesra, dedicated to promote the spirit of entrepreneurship among the students of the college and the region. We at EDC host various workshops, speaker sessions, innovative games, and competitions along with pitching sessions and networking opportunities for aspiring entrepreneurs and support them by providing necessary resources.
